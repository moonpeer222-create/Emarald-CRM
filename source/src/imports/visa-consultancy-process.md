🔄 Complete 12-Stage Workflow:
Stage 1: Lead Generation & Qualification 📱
Client Facebook post parh kar contact karta hai
Chatbot basic information leta hai + non-serious clients filter karta hai
Sales Rep ka target: 5 mature clients/day
Mature clients ko office visit ke liye schedule kiya jata hai
Stage 2: Office Visit & First Impression 🏢
Client office aata hai → pehle safai/decor dekhta hai
Staff ki discipline aur professionalism observe karta hai
Client check karta hai: "Kya mere ilawa bhi clients hain?" (trust building)
Expert/Main person se meeting hoti hai jo har sawal ka jawab de
Retainer Agreement sign karwaya jata hai (client-friendly terms)
Stage 3: Document Collection & Scanning 📄
Sab documents scan kiye jate hain:
CNIC, Passport (all pages), Photos (full body + passport size)
Driver/Operator ho to License bhi
Sab data properly save kiya jata hai (client name + date ke sath)
Stage 4: Medical Token & GAMCA Process 🏥
Medical Token issue kiya jata hai (Fee: 4,500 PKR - client pays)
Medical center fee: 25,000-35,000 PKR (client direct pay karta hai)
Client ko WhatsApp par guide kiya jata hai:
Lab address, route, required documents (passport copy + photos)
36 hours mein medical result aata hai
If Unfit: Case cancel + passport return
If Fit: Visa processing start immediately
Stage 5: E-Number & Vendor Submission 📤
Computer Operator PDF file prepare karta hai (36 hours before biometric)
File Owner ko bheji jati hai → Owner Vendor ko bhejta hai
E-Number WhatsApp par receive hota hai → Computer Operator ko share kiya jata hai
Client ko call: "Kal 2 lakh PKR le kar aayen (biometric + payment)"
Passport stock (Imran ke ghar) se mangwaya jata hai
Stage 6: Payment Collection (2 Lakh PKR) 💰
Cash: Receipt di jati hai + register mein entry
Online: Screenshot liya jata hai → Owner se confirmation → entry
Original Documents Received:
Old + New Passport, CNIC, Photos, FRC, PCC
2 Biometric Slips, Medical Report
License (if Driver/Operator)
⚠️ Warning: Agar ek bhi document missing → Embassy visa nahi degi
Stage 7: Case Registration & Vendor Handover 📝
Entry computer CRM + manual register dono mein
TCS slip (if sending documents) file mein attach
Note: Case kis vendor ke paas ja raha hai
File Owner ko handover ki jati hai
Stage 8: Vendor Payment & Visa Processing 💸
Owner vendor ko online payment karta hai
Payment record Computer Operator ko diya jata hai
Computer Operator har entry se pehle Owner se confirm karta hai
Daily vendor payment log maintain kiya jata hai
Vendor visa lagwa kar WhatsApp par copy bhejta hai → Owner → Computer Operator
Stage 9: Visa Approval & Remaining Payment 🎉
Staff client ko visa ki mubarakbad deta hai (WhatsApp/call)
Next day: Client ko remaining payment ke liye bulaya jata hai
Payment received → Owner se confirmation (accounts mein transfer hua ya nahi?)
Cash ho to receipt di jati hai
Owner ko remind kiya jata hai: "Is client se itne rate par deal hui thi"
Stage 10: Protector Process 🛡️
Client se Nominee details + Account number manga jata hai → save → Owner ko bheja jata hai
Owner Vendor se Protector Paper nikalwa kar deta hai
Client ko 8 AM Protector Office bheja jata hai
Client stamp lagwa kar original paper + 200 PKR guarantee stamp office mein submit karta hai
Client signature + thumb impression protector paper par lagwaya jata hai
Paper scan kar ke Owner ko diya jata hai → Vendor protector online kar deta hai
Stage 11: Ticket Booking & Final Handover 🎫
Agar full payment complete ho chuki hai:
Ticket date finalize ki jati hai
Vendor ko final payment complete ki jati hai
Complete file vendor se mangwayi jati hai
Client ki video statement record ki jati hai
File handover ki jati hai
Stage 12: Departure ✈️
Client fly karta hai → Alhamdulillah! 🎉
